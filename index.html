About Dhanaa

The Art & Science
of Premium Spices

Dhanaa Ceylon Spices was founded with a clear mission: to bring the world's finest Sri Lankan spices to international markets with the integrity and professionalism that premium buyers expect. We operate as a lean, expert-led export house — registered in Sri Lanka as Dhanaa Spices and in the United Kingdom as Dhanaa Ceylon Spices LTD.

Our operation spans the full supply chain: from direct relationships with certified farmers in Galle, Matara, Kandy, and Matale, through our controlled processing and packaging facility, to documentation management and global logistics coordination. We handle everything so our buyers receive a seamless, reliable supply.

What distinguishes Dhanaa is our commitment to transparency. Every shipment comes with a complete documentation package — Certificate of Analysis, Phytosanitary Certificate, Fumigation Certificate, Certificate of Origin, and full traceability records. We believe buyers should never have to chase paperwork.

Our Mission

To be the most trusted premium B2B spice exporter from Sri Lanka — delivering exceptional quality, complete transparency, and reliable supply to food businesses worldwide.

Our Vision

To position Sri Lankan spices — especially Ceylon cinnamon — as the global benchmark for quality, and to build Dhanaa as the export partner of choice for premium food manufacturers worldwide.

Core Values

Integrity in every transaction. Precision in every shipment. Sustainability in every sourcing decision. Partnership in every buyer relationship.

Our Process

From Harvest to
Your Warehouse

Every spice passes through a rigorous multi-stage quality control process. We don't just inspect the final product — we control quality at every point in the supply chain.

01
Farm-Level Sourcing
Direct partnerships with certified growers. Pre-harvest assessments and farmer training.
02
Raw Material Inspection
Incoming lots tested for moisture, volatile oil content, microbiological load, and physical defects.
03
Processing & Cleaning

Pre-Shipment Testing

Documentation Package
